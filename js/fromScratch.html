Why I Like Reading

I love reading because it is a very relaxing hobby. You get to enter into new worlds and do not have to worry about the stressors of life. Unlike TV or movies, which can also be a nice break or escape, your brain can still think during movies. With reading, your brain is creating the images you are reading from the page and making a world in your head. There is no room to stress or think about anything else when your brain is already working.

I also love to read and tan with my little sister in the summer. That makes reading even more enjoyable for me and turns it into one of my favorite ways to spend time.

How I Got Into Reading

I got into reading from a series called The Selection. It sparked my love for reading and made me want to keep finding more books and series that I would enjoy.
